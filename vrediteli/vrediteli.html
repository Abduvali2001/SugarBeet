Вредители

  • Spider mite

    The most harmful of these on sugar beets is currently the spider mite.

  • Beet flea

    The use of seeds treated with insecticides (insecticides are chemical means of protection against pests) eliminated the risk of damage to beets by the beet flea in the period from germination to 3-4 pairs of leaves.

  • Redhead

    A certain danger to beets is represented by gnawing and leaf-gnawing scoops.

  • Root aphid

    There is also a threat of damage to plants by leaf and root aphids.

  • Miner fly

    The beet miner fly also damages beet leaves.

  • Nematodes

    Among the most dangerous pests of sugar beet are nematodes - beet, gall, stem, etc. .

  • Stalked weevil

    Appears on sugar beets in late April - early May.

  • Winter scoop.

    The winter moth belongs to the gnawing moths and develops in 2 generations.
