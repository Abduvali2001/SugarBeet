Root aphid

Click to show the picture

There is also a threat of damage to plants by leaf and root aphids. On crops, especially during manual harvesting, a colony of root aphids on beetroots in the zone of root grooves is clearly visible. In addition, a white bloom that looks like mold is striking. This is their waxy discharge, among which these pests swarm up to 2 mm in size. This is a typical sucking soil habitat. Sucking the juices from the roots, they cause their death. It also propagates on crops of corn, roots of weeds from the family of haze weed (quinoa, white gauze, etc.). Losses in yield - up to 8-9 t / ha and sugar content - by 1-1.5%. The harmfulness is strongly manifested with a lack of moisture and heat.

Protection methods

Agrotechnical

Compliance with crop rotation. It is also necessary to keep the fields free of weeds.
