The procedure for preparing the working fluid

If you use a knapsack sprayer, follow the following rules for preparing the working fluid:

1. Fill solution tank halfway with clean water (no contaminants that could clog sprayer).

2. Accurately measure the drug according to the calibration.

3. Add the drug to the sprayer tank

4. Close the sprayer and mix the water with the preparation with light circular movements or move it through the opening with a stick. Never stir by hand!

5. Fill the tank to the top with water. Before putting on the sprayer, wipe it dry with a cloth!

Directions: For an accurate dosage of the drug, it is recommended to use a disposable syringe. The syringe must have a label indicating the purpose of its use!

Источник раздела: А.У. Джалилов, А.А. Вахидов, «Справочник по защите растений», 2010
