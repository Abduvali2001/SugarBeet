Measuring the amount of the drug

How to measure a small amount of the drug?

For an accurate dosage of the drug, it is recommended to use a disposable syringe. Difficulties arise when there is no way to accurately measure the powder preparation, there are no exact scales. In this case, you can use other simple methods.

  • Make a liquid out of the powder so that you can measure the drugs with a syringe in ml, and not by weighing. To do this, the drug must be mixed with a small amount of clean water.
  • If the weight of the drug is indicated on the package of the drug (for powder drugs), then they can be divided into parts: halves, quarters or eighths. This can be done on a plastic plate with a knife. If, for example, a package of pesticide powder is 100 grams, this package can be divided into two parts (2 X 50 g), four (4 X 25 g) or eight (8 X 12.5 g).
  • If you know the volume or weight of the preparation that a teaspoon or dessert spoon or matchbox can hold, then you can use it to measure the preparation.

Section source: A.U. Jalilov, A.A. Vakhidov, "Plant Protection Handbook", 2010
