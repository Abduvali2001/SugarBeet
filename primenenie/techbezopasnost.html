Safety

  • Care must be taken when working with pesticides and mineral fertilizers;
  • Pesticides and mineral fertilizers can lead to various diseases and death;
  • If pesticides and mineral fertilizers enter rivers and lakes through runoff, they will have a negative impact on fish;
  • Always wear gloves;
  • It is possible to enter the processed fields only after 2 days;
  • Pesticide treatments can be carried out no later than 20 days before harvest, otherwise the consumption of products from this field will have a negative impact on human health;
  • Processing should be carried out standing against the wind;
  • After working with pesticides and mineral fertilizers, wash all used accessories, as well as hands;
  • When working with pesticides and mineral fertilizers, the consumption of food and water is strictly prohibited;
  • Storage of pesticides and mineral fertilizers should be placed away from the source of fire and children;
  • During strong wind, rain and wind, it is not advisable to carry out processing;
  • Pesticide treatments must be carried out carefully so that they do not adversely affect other plant species.

Section source: A.U. Jalilov, A.A. Vakhidov, "Plant Protection Handbook", 2010
