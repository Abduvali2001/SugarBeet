The use of drugs

The optimal effectiveness of plant protection products depends on the quality of the preparation, the correct timing and the careful and skillful application of the preparation, in other words, on the correct application technology. This requires maintaining a good technical condition of the sprayer, compliance with the necessary application parameters and safety precautions for handling pesticides.

You should prepare in advance the tools that you may need. I must say that the common misfortune of all farmers is that when working with pesticide solutions, they use a variety of, sometimes completely unsuitable devices: hydraulic controls, brooms, brushes, and so on. In fact, if the treatment is not carried out with a tractor sprayer, it is recommended to use knapsack sprayers with a 1.2 m rod so that drops of the solution do not get on the skin, eyes or respiratory organs. The longer the sprayer boom, the better for the person spraying.

In addition, special clothing is needed to work with drugs:

  • A bathrobe (or overalls) that will prevent drops from penetrating the skin of the sprayer.
  • Rubber Shoes.
  • Hat or cap.
  • Rubber gloves.
  • If indicated in the instructions, goggles and respirators.
  • apron.

It is important to store all this special clothing separately from the rest of the clothing and not be lazy to wash it every time after working with pesticides, and with laundry soap..

So, you get to work. To do this, in most cases, the drug is first diluted with water. Of course, this is also done strictly according to the instructions, and most importantly - in special dishes, in no case in food.
