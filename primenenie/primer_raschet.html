Calculation example

To protect against the Colorado potato beetle, it is recommended to use the Confidor preparation, the consumption rate of which is 100 ml / ha.

Working fluid preparation:

The farmer sprays an area with a width of 10 meters and a length of 50 meters with one refill.

Length x Width = Sprayed area in m2
50 м х 10 м =500 м2

Calculation of the area for 1 gas station:

If 1 ha can be divided by the area sprayed by 1 sprayer load, then we get the number of sprayer loads per hectare:

Now we divide the drug consumption rate per 1 ha (100 ml) by the number of refills per 1 ha. Thus, we get the consumption rate per refueling:

So, in our example, for one refueling of the sprayer, you need to add 5 ml of Confidor.
