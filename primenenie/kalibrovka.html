The amount of the drug per 1 hectare is indicated in its description. The number of sprayer fillings per 1 hectare must be calculated. To find out how many refills are needed per 1 hectare, first measure the area that can be treated with one sprayer refill. The size of the area that can be treated with a single filling of the sprayer depends on many factors:

  1. on the amount of solution that is sprayed in a certain unit of time, which in turn depends on the size of the nozzle and the operating pressure;
  2. from the width of the spraying strip during operation;
  3. on the speed of movement of the person conducting the spraying.

Working method:

  1. fill the knapsack sprayer to the top with water;
  2. spray the test area with one dressing (the lane width is medium, the pace of movement is not fast);
  3. measure the treated area.
