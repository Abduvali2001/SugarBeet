Diseases

  • Powdery mildew

    Powdery mildew usually appears in mid-July in the form of a white bloom on the surface of the leaves (they seem to be dusted with flour); appears later in wet years.

  • Cercospora

    Cercosporosis - the source of infection can be not only beets, but also beetroot, quinoa, sorrel, alfalfa, etc.

  • Root rot

    Root rot can be one of the reasons for the decrease in yield and sugar content of sugar beets

  • Rhizomania

    Of the viral diseases, rhizomania (fibroses) is considered significant.
