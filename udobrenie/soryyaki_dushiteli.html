How to deal with the parasitic weed-strangler dodder?

КHow to deal with the parasitic weed-strangler dodder?

It is necessary to work against it constantly: observe crop rotation, use rotted manure, mow during the growing season, not allowing sowing, destroy on alfalfa (after mowing on 7-10 days, treat with 36% glyphosate 0.6-0.8 l / ha). On beets, the herbicide Kerb-50 is recommended in the phase of 2–3 pairs of true leaves at a dose of 4–5 kg / ha. There is also a second option: the introduction of Kerb-50 with a small dose of 0.5 kg / ha, starting from the moment the dodder germinates (has not yet managed to catch on to the plant), but this method has not yet been sufficiently studied in the conditions of Kyrgyzstan. Kerb-50, when applied, is combined in a tank mixture with a standard herbicidal treatment against other weeds. Since dodder emerges almost simultaneously with beets, and this process is delayed in time, 2-3 such treatments are necessary with an increase in the rate to 0.6-0.8 kg / ha.
